About Me

Having garnered significant experience as a Pharmacy Assistant and equipped with a solid health background, I am currently making a transition into the tech field through web development. This shift is driven by my enrollment in the DTTP (Digital Technology Training Program), where I am actively acquiring new skills and knowledge. My journey underscores a fusion of healthcare insights and a burgeoning interest in technology, as I strive to contribute to the dynamic landscape of web development. Embracing continuous learning, I am enthusiastic about bridging the gap between healthcare and digital innovation, bringing a unique blend of expertise to this evolving field.

The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, front-end and back-end development, responsive design, and the practical use of popular frameworks and libraries. This program not only hones technical proficiency but also emphasizes hands-on experience through practical projects, allowing me to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios. I've become adept at creating visually appealing and functional websites while gaining insights into web architecture and user experience.
